What is this website for?

This site helps users discover Korean dishes based on taste preferences such as spiciness, sweetness, and more. It's built as a personal project to explore how algorithms can enhance food recommendations.

How are dish suggestions made?

Suggestions are based on taste profiles. Dishes are categorized using structured data (like spiciness level or healthiness), and your selections help filter out the most relevant dishes for you.

Are the ratings and data accurate?

Most ratings are based on manually collected and organized data. While not crowd-sourced yet, they are structured and consistent for experimental recommendation logic.

Can I suggest a new dish or feature?

Yes! Your feedback is appreciated. Feel free to contact us via email with suggestions or new dish ideas you'd like to see.

Will more features be added?

This project is still evolving. Future ideas include user login, favorites, and more advanced recommendation logic based on review data and similarity algorithms.

How can I support or contribute?

This is an open portfolio project. If you're a developer, feel free to reach out for collaboration. Users can support by sharing the project or providing feedback.
